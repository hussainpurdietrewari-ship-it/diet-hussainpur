About DIET Hussainpur

Catalysing quality teacher education for vibrant classrooms.

Established in 1992, DIET Hussainpur anchors teacher education for Rewari district and adjoining blocks, delivering pre-service D.El.Ed. programmes, in-service professional development and research aligned with the National Education Policy 2020.

Principal's Message

A warm welcome to DIET Hussainpur, Rewari. Quality education will always remain the bedrock of national progress and teachers hold a permanent responsibility in shaping society. Guided by this belief, our faculty pursues every venture with commitment, blending innovative, activity-based and ICT-enabled practices across our spacious eight-acre campus.

As a premier institute of Haryana, we prepare both pre-service and in-service teachers through trainings, mentoring and meaningful research inputs for SCERT/NCERT. We pledge to continue making worthwhile contributions across every project we undertake and wish a bright career for each DIET student.

Sh. Subhash Chander Yadav
Principal, DIET Hussainpur

Vision & Mission

Foster competent, compassionate and tech-ready teachers.
Support school transformation through mentoring & action research.
Strengthen Foundational Literacy & Numeracy outcomes.
Model inclusive & green campus practices for the district.

Strategic Pillars

Academic Leadership

Outcome-focused D.El.Ed. curriculum delivery, reflective practicum and assessment reforms.

Research & Innovation

District learning studies, action research grants and dissemination of best practices via publications.

Field Interaction

Close coordination with BRC/CRC networks, school visits and School Development Plans monitoring.

Capacity Building

Continuous Professional Development aligned with NISHTHA, FLN and Foundational Stage reforms.

Timeline & Milestones

1992 · Inception

DIET Hussainpur notified by SCERT Haryana to serve Rewari & adjoining districts.

2004 · Residential Campus

Hostel facilities, science labs and playfields developed through RMSA support.

2015 · Smart Classrooms

Digital pedagogy lab, studio classroom and ICT resource centre inaugurated.

2020 onwards · NEP Response

FLN Mission cell, toy-based pedagogy lab, ECCE demonstration corners and hybrid training ecosystem built.

Academic & Support Infrastructure

Academic Block

Lecture galleries, Micro-teaching lab, Psychology lab and resource-rich library with 16,000 texts.

Experiential Labs

Science park, Robotics corner, Toy-based learning zone and Art-integrated education studio.

Residential & Sports

Separate hostels, multi-cuisine dining, 400m athletic track, volleyball and kabaddi courts.

Student Support

Wellness centre, counselling cell, innovation clubs and student parliament for governance.

Academic Programmes

Rigorous teacher education aligned with NEP 2020.

DIET Hussainpur delivers competency-based D.El.Ed. programme, in-service professional learning experiences and specialised certificates to strengthen foundational schooling.

Download Curriculum Guide

D.El.Ed. (Pre-Service) Overview

Two-year programme with integrated theory, practicum and school internship components.

Semester Key Courses Credits Highlights
I Childhood & Development, Language Education I, Mathematics Pedagogy 24 Foundation courses, school attachment (2 weeks)
II Arts & Culture, Environmental Studies, Assessment for Learning 22 Micro-teaching lab, community survey
III Inclusive Education, ICT in Education, Language Education II 23 Internship Phase I (4 weeks) & action research proposal
IV Yoga & Wellbeing, School Leadership basics, Contemporary Issues 21 Internship Phase II (8 weeks) with portfolio defence

In-Service Professional Development

NISHTHA 4.0 FLN

Hybrid delivery with DIKSHA modules, need-based tutorials and school mentoring visits.

Primary Maths Lab

30-hour experiential module on manipulative-based pedagogy and assessment design.

School Leadership

Two-week certification for head teachers focusing on instructional leadership practices.

Inclusive Classroom Series

Orientation on UDL, multi-grade teaching strategies and assistive technology integration.
